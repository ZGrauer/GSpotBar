Ping-Pong

The bar has a free to use ping-pong table. So, come to The G Spot and paddle some balls around.

8 Pool Table

A pool table is available for free to bar patrons. Go ahead and play with yourself on a game of nine-ball, or with a group on a game of eight-ball.

Cards Against Humanity

Gather your friends for a fun night of Cards Against Humanity at The G Spot. The bar has nearly every expansion pack filling the bigger blacker box.

Cards Against Humanity is a party game for horrible people. Unlike most of the party games you've played before, Cards Against Humanity is as despicable and awkward as you and your friends.

SNES Games

The G Spot is equipped with a Super Nintendo (SNES) with over 200 classic games for patrons to play for free. Challenge friends to games of Mario Kart, Mortal Kombat, or Turtles In Time.
